Our Mission

You Own It. We build AI software that belongs to the people who use it. Not to us, not to a cloud provider, not to a model.

Our Mandate

We give organisations back control over their AI. Software they can own, understand, and leave at any time. Data sovereignty is the foundation, not an add-on.

Our Vision

A future where organisations use AI without making themselves dependent. We are the proof that powerful AI and full sovereignty are not a contradiction.

Our measure: has the organisation that uses our software gained more control, more clarity, and more time for what matters? If yes, we have done our job well.
